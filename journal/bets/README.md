# Bet journal — plans & outcomes

Plans are created through conversation with MOST before placing a bet.

When you discuss a bet with the AI, it creates `YYYY-MM-DD-<event>-<market>.md` with:
- Selection, odds, stake, market, thesis, estimated probability, edge calculation
- Post-bet verification (PASS/MISMATCH)
- Settlement result (W/L/void), P/L in units, CLV, compliance tag

The AI compares your locked plan to what you actually placed — **neutral diff**, not approval/rejection.
