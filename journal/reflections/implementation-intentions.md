# Implementation Intentions

Pre-committed if-then plans. Written cold. Executed hot. Bypasses willpower.

Research: shifts goal attainment from ~22% to ~62% (Gollwitzer, 1999).

---

## Active Intentions

### After a loss
**If** my bet loses, **then** I will ___.

### Accumulator urge
**If** I feel the urge to place an accumulator, **then** I will ___.

### In-play impulse
**If** I see a live bet opportunity mid-match, **then** I will ___.

### Late night
**If** it is after ___ PM and I want to place a bet, **then** I will ___.

### First loss of session
**If** my first bet of the day loses, **then** I will ___.

### Chasing urge
**If** I feel the urge to bet bigger to recover losses, **then** I will ___.

### Phone betting
**If** I see odds on my phone and want to place a quick bet, **then** I will ___.

---

## How to use

- Fill these in during a calm session. Be specific — "close the betting app" not "take a break."
- AI will reference the specific intention when it detects the trigger.
- Update during calm/reflective sessions only. Never edit while activated.
- Add new intentions as new trigger patterns emerge.
