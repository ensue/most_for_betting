## The Patterns

_AI populates this section as behavioral patterns emerge from betting conversations. Common destructive patterns to watch for:_

### Chasing Losses
_Does stake size increase after losses? Are bets placed specifically to "recover"? Does the user switch to higher-odds markets or accumulators after a loss?_

### Accumulator Addiction
_Does the user default to parlays/accas over singles? Is there a daily accumulator habit? Does adding legs feel like "getting more value"?_

### In-Play Impulse
_Are live bets placed without pre-game analysis? Is the user reactive to game momentum? Does watching sport trigger betting impulses?_

### Sure-Thing Fallacy
_Does the user oversize on heavy favorites? Is "this can't lose" language present? Does confidence exceed calibrated probability?_

### Late-Night Betting
_Are bets placed outside planned session hours? Does bet quality drop after a certain time? Are these bets more impulsive?_

### Hot Hand Fallacy
_After a winning streak, do stakes increase? Do selection criteria loosen? Is there "I'm on a roll" language?_

### Confirmation Bias
_Is research one-sided — looking for reasons TO bet rather than reasons NOT to? Are contradictory stats ignored?_
